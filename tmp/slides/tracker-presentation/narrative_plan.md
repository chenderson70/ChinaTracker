# China Tracker Presentation Plan

## Audience
Senior decision-makers and stakeholders evaluating whether China Tracker is ready to be trusted for exercise budget planning and briefing support.

## Objective
Show that China Tracker replaces fragile spreadsheet-driven budgeting with a faster, cleaner, more auditable workflow that is ready for operational use and has clear expansion potential.

## Narrative Arc
1. Establish the mission and why this application matters.
2. Explain the workflow in simple, leadership-friendly language.
3. Show why the web app is stronger than Excel for this use case.
4. Highlight the features that matter most in live planning.
5. Prove it with real screenshots from the running product.
6. End on what the platform can become next.

## Slide List
1. Title and executive framing
2. How the app works
3. Why it is better than Excel
4. Main features
5. Live demonstration: dashboard and command view
6. Live demonstration: unit planning, reporting, and refinements
7. Future potential and improvement roadmap
8. Closing value statement

## Visual System
- Format: 16:9 widescreen
- Tone: executive, modern, calm, technically credible
- Palette: navy, slate, white, electric blue, teal accent
- Typography: Aptos family with strong hierarchy and generous spacing
- Layout style: premium briefing deck with rounded panels, subtle layering, high contrast, and framed app screenshots

## Source Plan
- Product overview and architecture: README.md
- Feature details and workflow: USER_GUIDE.md
- Strategic framing and future direction: ChinaTracker_PRD_v2.md
- Live visuals: screenshots captured from the running localhost application

## Asset Plan
- Real screenshots for dashboard, A7 unit page, reports, and refinements
- Clean geometry, badges, timeline steps, and comparison cards authored as editable PowerPoint objects

## Editability Plan
- All titles, bullets, labels, and comparison content remain editable text
- Screenshots are embedded as visuals only
- Roadmap and workflow visuals are built from native shapes and text objects
